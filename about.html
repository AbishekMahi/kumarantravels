About Kumaran Travels

Kumaran Travels, a travel company was started in 1970 in Madurai. For the past 43 years, the company has been operating with the mission of providing full service at low cost. Many spiritual tours were conducted with the blessings of Tirumuruga Kripanandavariyar. Awards for Best Service in Tourism were also presented at the function presided over by Madurai Mr. Adinam.

Appreciation Ceremony held on 03.02.2013 on behalf of Mr.Senthilkumar, Proprietor of Kumaran Travels for Dr. Thavathiru Kumaragurupara Swami at Kaumara Temple, Coimbatore.The reason for providing complete service at low cost without any attractive huge advertisements is continuously giving us large number of customers. And our specialty is the maturity to serve the customers with patience, the nature of providing full service at low cost, assurance, complete tour of all the parts of the tour plan as given, trust, currency, and the ability to deal with customers with patience and kindness.

  1. All 3 meals are provided on all our tours.
  2. All our tour programs will be presented in plain Tamil in a way that everyone can understand. So you can clearly understand the tour you are participating in, places to visit, fare details and other rules.
  3. 99% Tamil people participate in the tour and you will feel like traveling with one family. You will not feel isolated like many other companies.
  4. All our employees have been working with us for many years. So none of our staff will give you false information or false promises.
  5. Fair fares that also benefit the middle class.
