About Kumaran Travels

Kumaran travels is a travel company started in 1970 in Madurai. For the past 52 years, the company has been working on a mission to provide full service travels and tours on a low budget. With the blessings of Tirumuruga Kripanandavariyar, many spiritual tours were conducted. Awards for “Best Service in Tourism” was awarded at the function presided over by Madurai Mr. Adinam.
Since its founding in 1970, Kumaran Travels Company has operated both local and international excursions, earning the respect of its patrons during that time. The fact that our tour participants continue to book with us after 52 years of travel shows how devoted our clients have been to us. And the fact that they send all of their family members, friends, and acquaintances on vacation with us is evidence of our value, their faith in us, and our capacity for first-rate tourism.

On behalf of Mr. Senthilkumar, proprietor of Kumaran Travels, a ceremony of appreciation for Dr. Thavathiru Kumaragurupara Swami was performed on February 3, 2013, at Kaumara Temple in Coimbatore. The reason we consistently receive a big number of clients is because we offer a full range of services at a reasonable price without using any eye-catching, extensive advertising. And our speciality is the maturity to serve the customers with patience, the nature of offering comprehensive service at a cheap cost, assurance, a thorough tour of all the sections of the tour plan as specified, trust, currency, and the capacity to deal with customers with kindness and patience.
