About Kumaran Travels

Kumaran Travels, a travel company was started in 1970 in Madurai. For the past 43 years, the company has been operating with the mission of providing full service at low cost. Many spiritual tours were conducted with the blessings of Tirumuruga Kripanandavariyar. Awards for Best Service in Tourism were also presented at the function presided over by Madurai Mr. Adinam.
